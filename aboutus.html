About Us



Welcome to Car Working Tutorials, a leading company in the automotive education industry! Our rich history spans over two decades of dedication to providing top-notch car working tutorials and courses. It all began in 2001 when our visionary founder, John Anderson, a skilled automotive engineer, recognized the need for accessible and comprehensive learning resources for aspiring car enthusiasts and professional mechanics alike. Fueled by his passion for automobiles and teaching, he set out on a mission to bridge the knowledge gap and empower individuals with in-depth insights into the functioning of cars. Over the years, Car Working Tutorials has evolved and grown, constantly adapting to the ever-changing automotive landscape. Our team of expert instructors, comprising automotive engineers, industry professionals, and technical specialists, work tirelessly to develop courses that cover a wide spectrum of car models and systems. We take pride in staying up-to-date with the latest advancements in automotive technology to deliver relevant and cutting-edge content. Through the years, we've garnered a global community of learners who have benefitted from our courses to pursue careers in the automotive industry, enhance their DIY car repair skills, or simply satiate their curiosity about how cars work. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has earned us accolades, and we cherish the success stories of our students who have gone on to achieve greatness in their automotive endeavors. As we look ahead, we remain dedicated to our founding principles of knowledge-sharing and fostering a deep appreciation for the intricacies of cars. Our journey continues, and we are excited to embark on new ventures, embracing technological innovations to make learning even more accessible and engaging. Join us at Car Working Tutorials as we celebrate our history and look forward to a future filled with boundless possibilities in the dynamic world of automotive education. Together, let's unlock the mysteries of car mechanics and drive towards a brighter, more informed automotive community.
